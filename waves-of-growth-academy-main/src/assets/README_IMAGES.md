## How to Add Your Images:

### Step 1: Replace Placeholder Files
Replace these placeholder files with your actual images:

1. **Group 1 Exam Image**
   - File: `tnpsc-group1.jpg`
   - Location: `src/assets/tnpsc-group1.jpg`
   - Replace with: Your Group 1 exam image

2. **Group 2 Exam Image** 
   - File: `tnpsc-group2.jpg`
   - Location: `src/assets/tnpsc-group2.jpg`
   - Replace with: Your Group 2 exam image

3. **Group 4 Exam Image**
   - File: `tnpsc-group4.jpg` 
   - Location: `src/assets/tnpsc-group4.jpg`
   - Replace with: Your Group 4 exam image

### Step 2: Refresh the Page
After replacing the files:
1. Save all files
2. Refresh your browser
3. Navigate to: Activities → TNPSC Coaching
4. You should see your new images!
